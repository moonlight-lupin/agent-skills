# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all, regardless of level of experience, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion, nationality, or political affiliation.

## Our standards

**Expected behaviour:**

- Be kind and respectful to others
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy toward other community members

**Unacceptable behaviour:**

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sexual language or imagery
- Insults or derogatory comments
- Other conduct which could be considered unprofessional

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the repository owner via GitHub's reporting mechanisms. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all content in this repository, including issues, pull requests, comments, and wiki pages.
